Wash hands with soap and water regularly even if you haven't been outdoors.

When coughing or sneezing cover mouth with flexed elbow or tissue.

Avoid travelling if you have a cold or cough.

If you have a cold, cough or difficulty breathing seek help by staying indoors.

If you're couhing or sneezing wear a mask and know how to dispose of it after use.
